AREA 51

Area 51 documentaries

explore the classified US Air Force base's history with secret aircraft (U-2, Stealth) and UFO/alien conspiracy theories,
often featuring whistleblowers like Bob Lazar,
with popular series like "UFO Files" covering alleged alien reverse-engineering,
while films like Oren Peli's Area 51 (2015) offer horror/sci-fi takes, and resources like Wikipedia provide history on the base and related media.
